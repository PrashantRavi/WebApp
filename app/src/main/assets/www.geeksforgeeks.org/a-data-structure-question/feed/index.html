Insert() – Inserts a node in BST and maintains the balance
Delete() – Deletes a node in BST and maintains the balance
FindMax() – Traverse to the right most node in BST
FindMax() – Traverse to the left most node in BST
DeleteMin() –
Call FindMin()
Call Delete()
DeleteMax() – Same as DeleteMin()
